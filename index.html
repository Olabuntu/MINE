Who I Am

Hi, I'm Olabuntu Babatunde Afeez, a Graduate Research Intern at the International Institute of Tropical Agriculture (IITA), Ibadan, working under Dr. Agre Paterne in the Yam Breeding Unit. I'm a passionate biochemist and computational biologist with expertise in Molecular Genetics, Bioinformatics, Drug Discovery, and Artificial Intelligence in healthcare.
